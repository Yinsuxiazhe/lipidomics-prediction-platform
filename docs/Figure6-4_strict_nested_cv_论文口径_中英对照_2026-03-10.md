This document is intended to formalize manuscript-style wording for Figure 6-4. All primary findings are based on aggregated outer-test predictions from the strict nested cross-validation framework, without reverting to the earlier workflow that performed feature selection before validation on the full sample. If the earlier higher-scoring results are discussed in the manuscript or during presentation, they should be framed only as exploratory or development-stage internal validation rather than as the primary result or final performance claim.

## Results

在严格嵌套交叉验证分析中，共纳入 281 名受试者，其中 response 163 例、noresponse 118 例，并在 5 个外层测试折上汇总模型表现。Figure 6-4 展示的 ROC 曲线与性能指标均来自外层测试折预测，因此其含义更接近模型在未参与当前训练与调参样本中的泛化能力，而不是开发阶段的内部拟合表现。在这一口径下，clinical + lipid fusion 模型取得最高的 mean AUC（0.536），其后依次为 lipid-only 模型（0.534）、clinical baseline 模型（0.530）、expanded fusion 模型（0.526）和 expanded clinical 模型（0.501）。总体上，融合模型相较于 lipid-only 和临床基线模型仅表现出幅度有限的增益，提示脂质组信息可能提供了补充性信号，但这一增益在严格验证下仍较小。

从训练集与外层测试集的差异来看，模型复杂度增加并未带来更稳定的泛化表现。clinical baseline 模型的 mean train AUC 为 0.606，对应的训练-测试差值约为 0.077，提示其虽然区分能力有限，但过拟合程度相对较低。相比之下，lipid-only、clinical + lipid fusion、expanded fusion 和 expanded clinical 模型的 mean train AUC 分别为 0.789、0.806、0.850 和 0.744，而对应的训练-测试差值分别约为 0.256、0.269、0.324 和 0.243。尤其是 expanded fusion 模型虽获得了最高的训练集区分度，却未转化为最佳外层测试 AUC，说明更大特征空间和更高模型复杂度主要提高了样本内表现，而未同步改善样本外预测性能。

In the strict nested cross-validation analysis, 281 participants were included, comprising 163 responders and 118 non-responders, and model performance was summarized across five outer test folds. The ROC curves and performance metrics shown in Figure 6-4 were derived exclusively from outer-fold test predictions; accordingly, they are more informative about generalization to data not involved in model training and tuning than about development-stage in-sample fit. Under this framework, the clinical plus lipid fusion model achieved the highest mean AUC (0.536), followed by the lipid-only model (0.534), the clinical baseline model (0.530), the expanded fusion model (0.526), and the expanded clinical model (0.501). Overall, the fusion model showed only a limited advantage over the lipid-only and clinical baseline models, suggesting that lipidomic information may contribute complementary signal, but that the magnitude of this gain remained small under strict validation.

The comparison between training and outer-test performance further indicated that increasing model complexity did not yield more stable generalization. The clinical baseline model had a mean train AUC of 0.606, corresponding to a train-test gap of approximately 0.077, which suggests limited discrimination but comparatively less overfitting. In contrast, the lipid-only, clinical plus lipid fusion, expanded fusion, and expanded clinical models yielded mean train AUCs of 0.789, 0.806, 0.850, and 0.744, with corresponding train-test gaps of approximately 0.256, 0.269, 0.324, and 0.243, respectively. Notably, the expanded fusion model achieved the highest apparent discrimination in the training data but did not produce the best outer-test AUC, indicating that the larger feature space and higher model complexity mainly improved in-sample performance without a parallel improvement in out-of-sample prediction.

## Discussion

这些结果表明，当前数据中可能存在一定的预测信号，但其泛化能力仍然有限。尽管 clinical + lipid fusion 模型在数值上位居第一，其相对于 lipid-only 和临床基线模型的提升幅度非常小，说明脂质组学信息在现阶段更像是提供了有限的增量信息，而不是带来了性质上的性能跃升。与此同时，expanded clinical 与 expanded fusion 两类更复杂模型并未优于更精简的对应模型，反而表现出更大的训练-测试差值，这一模式支持如下判断：在当前样本量、终点定义和变量结构下，继续增加变量数量或提升模型复杂度，未必能够换来更可靠的外部泛化能力。

在同一 strict nested CV 框架下进行的 exploratory 诊断也提供了相似信号。更稀疏的小模型在某些设置下可以降低训练-测试差值，例如 ultra-sparse lipid-only 配置的 mean AUC 约为 0.540，mean gap 约为 0.155，而 compact fusion 配置的 mean AUC 约为 0.538，mean gap 约为 0.224，但这类改进总体幅度有限，并未改变“泛化增益较小”的总体结论。因此，后续更值得优先推进的方向应是预先定义的小模型和稳定特征模型，而不是在同一批样本上继续无边界地堆叠变量、算法或搜索空间。若需要在正文中讨论旧版更高分结果，更合适的写法是将其界定为 exploratory 或 development-stage internal validation，用于说明开发阶段曾观察到较强信号，而不应将其作为主结果来支撑最终性能结论。

These findings suggest that some predictive signal may be present in the current dataset, but that its generalizability remains limited. Although the clinical plus lipid fusion model ranked first numerically, its advantage over the lipid-only and clinical baseline models was very small, indicating that lipidomic information currently provides only modest incremental information rather than a qualitative improvement in predictive performance. At the same time, the expanded clinical and expanded fusion models did not outperform their more parsimonious counterparts and instead exhibited larger train-test gaps. This pattern supports the interpretation that, given the current sample size, endpoint definition, and feature structure, simply adding more variables or increasing model complexity is unlikely to produce more reliable external generalization.

Exploratory diagnostics conducted within the same strict nested CV framework conveyed a similar message. More parsimonious models could reduce the train-test gap under some settings; for example, the ultra-sparse lipid-only configuration yielded a mean AUC of approximately 0.540 with a mean gap of approximately 0.155, whereas the compact fusion configuration yielded a mean AUC of approximately 0.538 with a mean gap of approximately 0.224. However, these improvements were limited in magnitude and did not change the broader conclusion that the overall gain in generalization remained small. Accordingly, the next methodological priority should be pre-specified small models and stability-oriented feature sets rather than unbounded expansion of variables, algorithms, or search space within the same cohort. If the earlier higher-scoring results need to be mentioned in the manuscript, they would be more appropriately described as exploratory or development-stage internal validation, useful for documenting stronger signal during model development but not suitable as the primary evidence supporting final model performance.

## Limitations

本研究的主要限制在于，当前证据仍来自单队列内部的严格验证，而非独立外部验证。虽然 strict nested CV 能够更有效地控制信息泄漏并减少性能乐观偏差，但它仍不能替代外部数据集上的独立检验。因此，现阶段更合理的定位是“在严格内部验证下观察到有限预测信号”，而不是“已经建立可稳定推广的预测模型”。此外，本研究样本量为 281 例，事件信息相对有限，不同模型之间的 AUC 差异较小，fold-level 波动亦较明显，这使得模型排序和性能差异应被谨慎解读。

另一项重要限制是终点与标签本身可能存在异质性或噪音。若 response/noresponse 的定义受到随访窗口、临床判断边界或数据整理过程的影响，则即便采用更复杂的算法，也难以在外层测试折中获得明显更高且更稳定的 AUC。基于这一原因，当前结果尚不足以支持强预测性结论，也不宜对稳定入选特征作过度生物学阐释。更稳妥的做法是将这些重复出现的特征视为后续小模型或稳定特征模型的候选变量，并在更严格、最好包含外部验证的数据框架中进一步检验。

The principal limitation of this study is that the current evidence is still derived from strict internal validation within a single cohort rather than from independent external validation. Although strict nested cross-validation is more effective at controlling information leakage and reducing optimistic bias, it cannot substitute for testing in an external dataset. Therefore, the most appropriate interpretation at this stage is that limited predictive signal was observed under rigorous internal validation, rather than that a model with stable transportable performance has already been established. In addition, the sample size was 281, the effective event information was limited, the differences in AUC across models were small, and fold-level variability remained evident, all of which mean that model ranking and apparent performance differences should be interpreted cautiously.

Another important limitation is the possibility of heterogeneity or noise in the endpoint and labeling process itself. If the definition of response versus non-response is influenced by follow-up window differences, boundary effects in clinical judgment, or data curation variability, then even more complex algorithms may struggle to achieve materially higher and more stable AUCs in outer-fold testing. For this reason, the current results do not support a strong predictive claim, and the repeatedly selected features should not yet be over-interpreted biologically. A more cautious approach is to regard these recurrent features as candidate variables for future parsimonious or stability-oriented models and to evaluate them further under a stricter framework, ideally including external validation.
